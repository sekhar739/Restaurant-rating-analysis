# Restaurant Ratings Analysis | Power BI Dashboard Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes restaurant ratings collected from consumers in Mexico, along with demographic information, dining preferences, restaurant characteristics, and customer reviews. The objective is to uncover consumer behavior patterns, restaurant performance trends, and factors influencing customer satisfaction.

## Business Problem

Restaurant owners and stakeholders need actionable insights into customer preferences, dining habits, and satisfaction levels to improve service quality, optimize operations, and enhance customer experience.

## Key Business Insights

### Customer Demographics

* Young adults represent the largest customer segment.
* Students form a significant portion of restaurant consumers.
* Public transportation is the most commonly used travel method.

### Restaurant Performance

* Mexican cuisine is the most preferred cuisine among consumers.
* Restaurants offering better food quality consistently receive higher overall ratings.
* Highly rated restaurants demonstrate strong food and service satisfaction scores.

### Operational Insights

* Most restaurants do not provide parking facilities.
* Non-smoking restaurants dominate the market.
* Restaurants serving alcohol generally receive positive customer feedback when food and service quality are maintained.

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus on improving food quality, as it has the strongest impact on customer satisfaction.
2. Expand popular cuisine offerings based on customer preferences.
3. Enhance service quality to improve repeat customer visits.
4. Consider parking facilities in high-demand locations.
5. Use customer demographic insights for targeted marketing campaigns.
